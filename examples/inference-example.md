## Context

Two video sources confirmed the same pattern. Puru shipped 29 free Claude skills as a public GitHub repo and drove TikTok funnel via "comment send" DMs. Vaibhax Sisinty followed with a top-10 review video naming front-end-design (Anthropic official) at 277K installs as the anchor pick. Same audience, same conversion pattern, two different creators.

## Inference

Skill packs are: portable, productized, demoable in seconds, low cost to author. For the Peer Operator audience (solo founders / multi-product builders / operators-who-build), a free skill pack outperforms PDFs, newsletters, and lead-gen calculators as a top-of-funnel asset. The pack converts because it solves a real problem on day one, not because the funnel pressures the prospect.

## Why this over alternatives

Email-gated PDF: low intent capture. Free calculator: doesn't compound beyond one use. Free course: too much commitment to consume. Skill pack: install, get value, remember who built it the next time you need governance for the stack.

## What NOT to lift

- The 277K-install number on front-end-design ≠ 277K useful installs (many are install-and-abandon)
- Puru's DM-funnel mechanics depend on platform attention dynamics that change; the SKILL pack pattern transfers, the DM choreography may not
- Don't conflate "free + valuable" with "free + flashy"; the valuable beats the flashy after the install
